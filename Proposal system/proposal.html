Frequently asked questions

The real estate industry is incredibly competitive and first impressions are priceless! Buyers who walk into an empty house, generally cannot see themselves living in the property, as it is not furnished thus can come across unappealing. Furnished homes offer buyers a sense of belonging and homeliness in a property. For this reason, professionally staged homes have a significant advantage in a crowded market. On average, professionally staged homes sell for 7.5% to 12.5% than those properties that are not staged.

A free quote is a detailed estimation provided by our team outlining the recommended staging package, room-by-room items, and pricing customized for your property campaign without any cost or obligation.

Simply contact us via phone or email, or submit the request form on our portal. We will arrange a brief site visit or analyze your floor plans to prepare your customized proposal.

Our standard packages include a six-week hire period, which covers a standard four-week marketing campaign plus one week for photography/setup and one week for scheduled collection. Extensions are available on a weekly basis.

Sale by Design Homes is fully insured. Our professional delivery and installation teams exercise the utmost care during setup and collection to ensure your property remains pristine and undamaged.
